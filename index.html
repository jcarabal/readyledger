Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Ready Ledger

‚ö†Ô∏è What happens to my data if I lose my device? +
IMPORTANT: If iCloud sync is NOT enabled and your device is lost, broken, stolen, or locked out, ALL your data will be lost forever. We strongly recommend enabling iCloud sync in Settings to automatically backup your data to your personal iCloud account. With iCloud sync enabled, you can restore your data on a new device by signing in with the same Apple ID.
Is my data really private? +
Yes! Ready Ledger stores all your data locally on your device. We don't have servers, we don't collect your data, and we can't see what you're tracking. Optional iCloud sync uses your private Apple account.
Do I need internet to use Ready Ledger? +
No! Ready Ledger works completely offline. All features are available without an internet connection. You only need internet if you choose to enable optional iCloud sync.
Can I use Ready Ledger without a bank account? +
Absolutely! Ready Ledger doesn't connect to banks at all. You manually enter your financial data, which makes it perfect for students, people without bank accounts, or anyone who prefers hands-on tracking.
Is Ready Ledger good for teaching financial literacy? +
Yes! Ready Ledger is designed with education in mind. Students can practice real-world financial scenarios in a safe, private environment without risking actual money or personal data.
What makes Ready Ledger different from other finance apps? +
Ready Ledger is privacy-first, offline-first, and combines career tracking with financial management. Unlike automated apps, manual tracking helps you build financial awareness and understanding.
Can I export my data? +
Yes! Your data is yours. You can export all your information in standard formats whenever you want.
